Shipping Policy

Fulfillment Timeline

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days of receipt.

Delivery Timeline

Orders within India are delivered within 3-5 business days.

Delivery Partners

We use IndiaPost Speed Post, Delhivery, and DTDC for domestic shipping.

Shipping Costs

Free shipping is available for all orders within India.

International Shipping: We ship to the USA, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Philippines, Malaysia, Thailand, Singapore, South Korea, and the European Union. A minimum order of 10 pieces is required, and shipping charges will be calculated. Please email us at support@qatalyst.zohodesk.in before placing an international order.

Order Tracking

All orders come with a tracking ID, which will be shared via email.

Incorrect Address

If an incorrect address is provided, we will attempt to rectify the issue with our delivery partner. However, we are not liable for replacements in such cases.
